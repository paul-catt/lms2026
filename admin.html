Fix a wrong score or completely undo a result. Correction overwrites the score and recalculates points. Undo wipes the result entirely.

⚠️ Danger Zone

Reset Results: Clears all pick_results and resets all match statuses to scheduled. Does not delete players, picks, teams or fixtures.

Match Reset: Clears all results, pick_results, eliminations, round snapshots, and round completion flags. Deletes all knockout matches (R32+). Keeps group stage fixtures intact. Resets all teams to not-eliminated.

☢️ Nuclear Reset (testing only): Wipes everything — results, pick_results, picks, goals predictions, eliminations, round snapshots, round completion flags. Deletes all knockout matches. Keeps group stage fixtures and player accounts. Use only during testing.
