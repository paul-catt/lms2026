🏆 How It Works

Each player picks teams each round and earns points based on their results. The player with the most points at the end of the tournament wins. Pick wisely — each team can only be used once across the entire competition.

You have a unique personal link to access your dashboard, submit picks, and track your score. The live leaderboard and match centre are available to everyone — no login needed.

🔵 Group Stages

There are three group phases across 11–27 June. Each phase you pick two teams: one as your 3-point pick and one as your 2-point pick. A win earns those points, a draw earns 1pt regardless of pick type, a loss earns 0. The 3-point pick is only available in group stages.

🔴 Knockout Stages

From the Round of 32 through to the Final, you pick one team per round. All knockout picks are worth 2 points for a win. Scoring for each outcome:

  • Your team wins — 2pts (+ longshot bonus if applicable)
  • Your team wins in Extra Time or on Penalties — 1pt (+ longshot bonus if applicable) — you stay in
  • Your team loses in Extra Time or on Penalties — 1pt — but you're eliminated
  • Your team loses — 0pts — and you're eliminated

⭐ Longshot Bonus

The bottom 24 teams by tournament odds are designated as longshots, marked with ⭐ in the pick form. If a longshot team wins — including in Extra Time or on Penalties in knockout rounds — the player who picked them earns an extra +1 point on top. The bonus applies in both group and knockout stages.

⏰ Deadlines & Advance Picks

Each round has a pick deadline — typically the morning before the first game of that round. Picks lock automatically at the deadline. No exceptions.

You can submit picks for future rounds in advance at any time, and change them freely up until that round's deadline. Your personal dashboard shows all open rounds with a dropdown to switch between them.

If you miss a deadline, you will be auto-assigned teams from the default pool in alphabetical priority order — two teams for group phases (3-point first, then 2-point), one for knockout rounds. Only teams still alive in the tournament and not already used by you are eligible. The pool teams are:

💀 Elimination

You are eliminated in any of the following situations:

  • Your picked team loses in the knockout phase (including in extra time or on penalties)
  • You have used all remaining teams and cannot make any more picks
  • You miss a knockout deadline and there are no default pool teams left that are eligible for you

In all cases, your score is frozen at the point where your final pick was used.

Example: Lee wins his semi-final pick, but has no eligible teams left for the final — he's eliminated after the semi-final and his score is frozen there. Hard luck, Lee.

🎯 Goals Prediction

At the start of the competition, each player submits a prediction for the total number of goals scored across all 104 matches, including goals in extra time. This is used as a tiebreaker — the player closest to the actual total wins the tiebreak. You can set or update your prediction from your personal dashboard during Group Phase 1.

🔢 Tiebreakers

If players are level on points at any stage, the following tiebreakers apply in order:

  • LMS — furthest round reached
    The player who has survived to the furthest knockout round ranks higher.
  • Goal difference from picked games
    Cumulative goal difference from all matches involving your picked teams. Higher is better.
  • Total goals prediction
    Closest to the actual total goals scored in the tournament (including extra time). Submitted at the start of the competition. Lower difference wins.
