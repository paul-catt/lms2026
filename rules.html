🏆 How It Works

Each player picks teams each round and earns points based on their results. The player with the most points at the end of the tournament wins. Pick wisely — each team can only be used once across the entire competition. With 3 group phases (2 picks each) and 5 knockout rounds (1 pick each), you have 11 picks in total.

You have a unique personal link to access your dashboard, submit picks, and track your score. The live leaderboard and match centre are available to everyone — no login needed.

⚽ Scoring

A 3-point pick on a longshot that wins earns 4 points. A 2-point pick on a longshot that wins earns 3 points.

🔵 Group Stages

There are three group phases. Each phase you pick two teams: one as your 3-point pick and one as your 2-point pick. A win earns those points, a draw earns 1pt regardless of pick type, a loss earns 0. The 3-point pick is only available in group stages.

🔴 Knockout Stages

From the Round of 32 through to the Final, you pick one team per round. All knockout picks are worth 2 points for a win. Scoring for each outcome:

  • Win at 90 mins — 2pts (+ longshot bonus if applicable)
  • Win via ET / penalties — 1pt (+ longshot bonus if applicable)
  • Loss via ET / penalties — 1pt, and you're out of the competition
  • Loss at 90 mins — 0pts, and you're out of the competition

⭐ Longshot Bonus

The bottom 24 teams by tournament odds are designated as longshots, marked with ⭐ in the pick form. If a longshot team wins — including via ET or penalties in knockout rounds — the player who picked them earns an extra +1 point. The bonus applies in both group and knockout stages.

⏰ Deadlines & Advance Picks

Each round has a pick deadline — typically the morning before the first game of that round. Picks lock automatically at the deadline. No exceptions.

You can submit picks for future rounds in advance at any time, and change them freely up until that round's deadline. Your personal dashboard shows all open rounds with a dropdown to switch between them.

💀 Elimination

You are eliminated if your team loses in the knockout phase. You are also out if you cannot pick a team — because every team remaining has already been used by you in a previous round. In either case, your score is frozen and your final position is locked at that point.

🔢 Tiebreakers

If players are level on points at any stage, the following tiebreakers apply in order:

  • LMS — furthest round reached
    The player who has survived to the furthest knockout round ranks higher.
  • Goal difference from picked games
    Cumulative goal difference from all matches involving your picked teams. Higher is better.
  • Total goals prediction
    Closest to the actual total goals scored in the tournament. Submitted at the start of the competition. Lower difference wins.
