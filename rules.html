World Cup 2026 Last Man Standing

🏆 How It Works

Each player picks teams each round and earns points based on their results. The player with the most points at the end wins. Pick wisely — each team can only be used once across the entire competition.

⭐ Longshot Bonus

The bottom 24 teams by tournament odds are designated as longshots. If a longshot team wins their match, the player who picked them earns an extra +1 point (before the multiplier is applied).

Longshot teams are marked with ⭐ in the pick form. The list is set before the tournament and can be adjusted by the admin if odds change significantly.

Longshot bonus applies in both group and knockout stages.

⏰ Deadlines

Each round has a pick deadline — typically the morning before the first game of that round. Picks lock automatically at the deadline. No exceptions.

If you miss the deadline, you will be auto-assigned a team from the default pool in priority order:

1.🇲🇽 Mexico
2.🇺🇾 Uruguay
3.🇺🇸 USA
4.🇯🇵 Japan
5.🇭🇷 Croatia
6.🇪🇨 Ecuador
7.🇸🇳 Senegal
8.🇸🇪 Sweden
9.🇦🇹 Austria
10.🇲🇦 Morocco

The first available team from this list that you haven't already used will be assigned. If all default pool teams are exhausted, the next available team from 40/1+ odds will be used.

In group phases, a missed deadline means you get one auto-assigned team as your 2-pointer only.

💀 Elimination

If you run out of teams — i.e. you have used all 48 teams — your score is frozen and you are out of the competition. Your final position is locked at that point.

🔢 Tiebreakers

If players are level on points, the following tiebreakers apply in order:

  • Longest in competition
    The player who has been in the competition longest (furthest round reached) ranks higher.
  • Most knockout points
    Points earned in knockout rounds only.
  • Goal difference from picked games
    Cumulative goal difference from all matches involving your picked teams.
  • Total goals prediction
    Closest to the actual total goals scored in the tournament. Submitted at the start of the competition.
